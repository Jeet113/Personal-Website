Hall Management System

A Smart Hall Management System is a digital solution designed to automate and simplify the management of hostels or dormitories. It replaces manual record-keeping and administrative tasks with a centralized, user-friendly platform for both students and administrators.

Echoes Of CUET

Echoes of CUET is a community-driven, map-based web application designed to preserve and share the memories of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology (CUET). The platform allows current students and alumni to post stories, images, and experiences linked to specific locations within the CUET campus. By combining interactive maps with user-generated content, the project creates a living digital archive of campus life.
